YOUR STUDIO
A SPACE FOR PASSIONATES AND CREATORS
We are a global video game development studio established in 2002. Headquartered in Warsaw with offices
in Cracow and Wrocław — as well as Los Angeles and Vancouver — with presence in 8 countries, the studio is
formed by over 1000 talented professionals hailing from all parts of the world. We’re independent and take
great pride in maintaining full creative control of the games we make. We consider ourselves rebels: we’re
intrepid and not afraid to experiment, we innovate and provoke change by giving example. Our mission is
to make the best role-playing games in the world and we will overcome every obstacle to make that a reality.
